Voter Education Center

Understand every step before election day: registration, verification, voting process, and your rights.

1) Registration & Verification

  • Register with your correct ID number and location details.
  • Complete OTP verification to activate your voter account.
  • Use your verified account to access your voting dashboard.

2) Positions You Vote For

  • President (national ballot).
  • Governor, Senator, Women Representative (county ballot).
  • Member of Parliament (constituency ballot).
  • MCA (ward ballot).

3) How Voting Works Here

  • Select a position from your dashboard.
  • Review candidate details before making a choice.
  • Confirm your vote on the confirmation page.
  • Each position can only be voted once.

4) Voter Rights & Good Practice

  • Your vote is secret and should never be disclosed under pressure.
  • Do not share your login credentials or OTP code.
  • Confirm candidate name and party carefully before final submission.
  • Report suspicious activity to election officials immediately.

Election Day Checklist

  • Ensure your account is verified before election day.
  • Log in early and review each ballot position carefully.
  • Vote once per position and verify your final confirmation message.
  • Keep your phone available in case any verification step is needed.

This guide is for voter awareness inside the system and does not replace official IEBC legal notices.
